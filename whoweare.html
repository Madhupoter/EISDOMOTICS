who we are

Home is where Comfort is
The Real Comfort is in Living Smart & Simple

As consumer demand for home automation and technology grows, it has become increasingly crucial to consider a home technology system during the planning stage of the residence/ project rather than an afterthought. Planning in advance for the home automation, audio- video , private-theatre, home networking and the whole house integration systems, is guaranteed to add value to the home and will save you from the stress of a complex retrofit installation later. Our passion for high quality & sophisticated luxury smart homes is what drives us everyday.

Whether you're considering a surround-sound system, a state-of-the-art security system or an automation system, purchasing technology for your home is unlike many other purchases you might make.

Since these electronic devices work as a system, pricing them can be a bit more difficult. For example, if you want to buy a car, you can walk up and look at the sticker price; but if you had to buy the seats, motor, doors, windows, tires and other parts separately, the process would be more complex. Fortunately, a good home technology professional can help you navigate these waters - designing a system that meets both your budget and performance requirements.

As a specialised home technology consultant and installer , we specialise in designing and installing a full range of home technology systems, including [home cinema, lighting control, curtain control, electronic security, networking , and multi-room audio]. To top all of this, all the pieces need to play nicely together as a single integrated system.

WHAT CAN WE DO FOR YOU?

Home automation that provides control of lighting, audio/video, motorised blinds and curtains, pool pumps and heaters, air conditioners, irrigation systems and more.

Audio and Video storage and playback systems to allow movies and music to be shared and streamed throughout the home.

Integrated Security, door entry, intercom systems that deliver the highest level of safety and convenience.

Discreet whole-home audio systems that offer room-by-room listening experience without ugly hi-fi components and cumbersome speakers.

Energy management solutions that ensure efficient use of electricity, thereby minimising wastage and reducing greenhouse emissions.

Home cinema design from acoustic treatment, to technology integration for immersive audio formats such as Dolby ATMOS, Auro-3D.

Data networks that provide distribution of broadband, telephone and CCTV.

Provision to upgrade to emerging technologies such as Voice Control, Virtual Reality Experience, 4K HDR.
